MailClicker

Welcome to Mail Clicker, the frantic clicking game where your goal is to clear your inbox of unwanted emails!

Your inbox is overflowing with spam, useless newsletters, and malicious emails. Your mission? Click to delete them, unlock automated tools, and become a master of digital organization!

How to play?

Click on the emails to delete them and earn clicks. Use your clicks to unlock upgrades and boosts to help you delete emails faster.

Passive Upgrades:

Leaf: Delete 1 email/sec

Sprout: Delete 5 emails/sec

Bush: Delete 25 emails/sec

Tree: Delete 50 emails/sec

Forest: Delete 100 emails/sec

Boosts:

+5 Click Boost: Multiply the number of emails deleted per click for 30 seconds (1-minute cooldown)

x2 Passive Income Boost: Increase passive income for 1 minute (2-minute cooldown)

Auto Clicker: Automatically delete 10 emails/sec for 20 seconds (3-minute cooldown)

Total Deletion Boost: Delete an additional 15% of the total emails already deleted (4-minute cooldown)

An addictive game!

Earn clicks to unlock upgrades and boosts.

Constant progression!

Optimize your cleaning with increasingly powerful tools!

Upcoming Features in the Future

Random Events

In MailClicker, you may encounter random events that can either help or hinder your progress. Watch out for the paper airplane that crosses the screen!

Helpful Events:

Government Grant: Triples the number of clicks for 5 minutes

The Eco-Friendly: Removes the cooldown from one of your boosts.

Harmful Events:

Deforestation: You lose 4 levels of your Forest upgrade.

Main Server Outage: A server goes down, causing a temporary reduction of 50% in clicks per second for 10 seconds.

Green Friday: Reduces passive upgrades by 15% for 30 seconds.
